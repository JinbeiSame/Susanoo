Outline

Susanoo project aims at developing a framework for sustainable management and optimal operation of dicentralized data centers. Our framework considers utlizing distributed natural resources, such as solar power and outdoor air, for sustainable management of the data centers. The framework provides a function to estimate performance and consumption of green energy/brown energy of the data centers in changing configuration of the data centers via migrating virtual machines, shutting down servers, and so on. This functions makes it possible to determine optimal operations of the data centers.
